🏗️ Model Architecture

Input (224 × 224 × 3 RGB) ↓ EfficientNetB3 (ImageNet weights, pooling='max') ↓ BatchNormalization (momentum=0.99, ε=0.001) ↓ Dense(256, relu) — L1/L2 Regularization ↓ Dropout(0.45) ↓ Dense(4, softmax)
